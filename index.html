How It Works

Our Building Process

A transparent, step-by-step journey — from your first call to receiving your keys.

01

Site Visit & Consultation

Our engineer visits your plot, understands soil type, faces the sun direction, and discusses your vision, budget, and requirements in detail — free of charge.

02

Design & Estimation

Vastu-compliant floor plans, 3D elevations, and a detailed cost estimate are provided within 5 working days — completely transparent with no hidden charges.

03

Approvals & Permits

We handle DTCP / Panchayat / Municipality plan approvals, CMDA submissions, and structural engineer certifications on your behalf across TN & Kerala.

04

Construction & Handover

Stage-by-stage construction with weekly photo updates, quality checks at every milestone, and a final walk-through before we hand you your dream home.
